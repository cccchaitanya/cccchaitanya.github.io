Values Of Prema:

Prema is a Sanskrit word meaning love or affection. Prema is a Sanskrit word for love. More specifically, it refers to pure, unconditional love that leads to total devotion and surrender.

Shanthi provide us with values such as:

  • Rather than selfish love(kama), prema refers to a divine love or higher love, related to God or some form of spiritual pursuit,
  • It helps people to be affectionate with each other.
