Values Of Shanthi:

Shanthi is a Sanskrit term meaning peace or inner peace. It is prayed at the end of an Upanishad. In Sanskrit, the ancient Indian language, Om Shanti means eternal peace for all of mankind. According to Hindu scripture, Shanthi is achieved only when a soul reaches moksha, the divine release from the endless cycle of reincarnation after living a righteous and pious life.

Shanthi provide us with values such as:

  • Can obtain inner peace,
  • Attain calmness,
  • Everlasting tranquility.
