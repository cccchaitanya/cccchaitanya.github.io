Values From Vedas

Values Of Dharma:

In Vedas and Upanishads, the concept of dharma continues as universal principle of law, order, harmony, and truth. It acts as the regulatory moral principle of the Universe. It is explained as law of righteousness.

Dharmas provide us with values such as:

  • An individual's duty fulfilled by observance of custom or law,
  • The basic principles of cosmic or individual existence(i.e) divine law,
  • Conformity to one's duty and nature.
