Introduction To Vedas:

The ancient sacred texts of Hinduism are called the Vedas which means knowledge. For a long time, they passed down the texts of the Veda by reciting them. Hindus today still study the Veda.

  • The Rigveda, contains more than 1,000 songs and poems about Hindu gods and rituals.
  • The Yajurveda helped priests follow these rituals.
  • The Samaveda contains verses that priests chanted or sang.
  • The Atharvaveda includes magic spells and rituals.
